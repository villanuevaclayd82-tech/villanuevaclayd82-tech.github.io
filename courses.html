Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

The BSIT program focuses on the study of computer systems, programming, and technology solutions. It prepares students for careers in software development, web design, networking, and database management.

Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM)

The BSHM program trains students in hotel, restaurant, and tourism management. It develops skills in customer service, event planning, and business operations in the hospitality industry.

Bachelor of Industrial Technology (BIT)

The BIT program offers hands-on training in technical and industrial fields. It prepares students to become skilled professionals in areas like electronics, automotive, and drafting technology.

Major in Computer Technology (ComTech)

The BIT Major in Computer Technology focuses on computer hardware, software applications, and technical troubleshooting. It equips students with practical knowledge in computer maintenance, networking, and IT support systems, preparing them for careers as computer technicians, IT support staff, and system operators.
